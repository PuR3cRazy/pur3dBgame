Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy describes how the "How Loud?!" app ("the app") handles information when you use it.

Microphone access

The app uses your device's microphone to measure ambient sound volume in real time. This is required for the app's core function: displaying an estimated decibel level and showing related commentary.

  • Audio is processed entirely on your device, in real time.
  • No audio is recorded, saved, stored, or transmitted anywhere.
  • No audio data leaves your device at any point.
  • The app does not have access to your microphone unless you have actively granted permission, and you can revoke this permission at any time through your device settings.

Data collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics tracking, and no third-party data sharing.

Local storage

Any session data (such as your peak volume reading) is kept temporarily in your device's memory while the app is open and is cleared when you close the app. Nothing is saved permanently or sent off your device.

Third-party services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, since it does not collect or store data at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this same URL with an updated "last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the app's listing on Google Play.
